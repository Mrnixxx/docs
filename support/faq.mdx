## General

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is carrotfunding.io in a nutshell?">
    carrotfunding.io is the next-generation trader funding firm built entirely on on-chain infrastructure. It gives both experienced and novice traders access to a proprietary simulated trading environment, and the opportunity to monetize their account after successfully passing a performance-based evaluation.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is a trader funding firm?">
    A trader funding firm provides capital to traders who demonstrate skill and consistency through a structured evaluation. Instead of risking your own funds, you trade with firm-provided capital and share a percentage of the profits you generate.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is carrotfunding.io's business model?">
    When you purchase an evaluation plan, 50% goes to carrotfunding.io (covering technology infrastructure, operational overhead, and platform development) and 50% goes to an on-chain vault on rethink.finance (which backs funded traders' accounts and supports payouts).

    Once you are funded, you earn 80% of profits and the remaining 20% goes to the vault. carrotfunding.io acts as fund manager, taking a 1% management fee and 20% performance fee — both subject to a high-water mark.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is carrotfunding.io trustworthy?">
    Yes. Unlike traditional prop firms that rely on opaque infrastructure, carrotfunding.io uses on-chain infrastructure for full transparency. You do not need to rely on trust — you can verify everything yourself.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need to deposit any money to get started?">
    No. You only pay a one-time evaluation fee when purchasing an evaluation plan. There are no deposits of trading capital required and no hidden fees.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Who can join carrotfunding.io?">
    Anyone who is 18 years or older and legally permitted to trade in their jurisdiction. carrotfunding.io welcomes traders from all backgrounds — DeFi-native, CEX, or just starting out. No prior trading history is required.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What challenge plans can I choose from?">
    Currently, carrotfunding.io offers the **Carrot 2-Phase Challenge**, which consists of an Evaluation phase followed by a Verification phase. When purchasing, you configure your plan by selecting an account size ($5k–$50k) and a leverage mode (Normal or Aggressive).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Evaluation & verification accounts

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is a Carrot Evaluation Account?">
    A simulated trading account that serves as the first step in the challenge. You trade in a simulated crypto market environment, and your performance is assessed against specific Trading Objectives: a profit target, drawdown limits, and a consistency score. There is no time limit.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is a Carrot Verification Account?">
    The second and final step before receiving a Carrot Funded Trader Account. Trading objectives in this phase are more relaxed — the profit target is reduced — but the same drawdown rules apply. This phase confirms the consistency of your trading performance. There is no time limit.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does it take to receive my Verification Account after passing Evaluation?">
    Instantly. The process is fully automated. As soon as you complete all Trading Objectives in the Evaluation phase, your Verification Account is created and appears in your dashboard immediately.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Where can I track my trading objectives?">
    In your Trader Dashboard. All data is automatically refreshed every 30 seconds.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does it take to receive my Funded Account after passing Verification?">
    Our team reviews your performance to confirm compliance with trading rules — this typically takes 1–2 business days. Once approved, your Carrot Funded Trader Account appears in your Trader Dashboard.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Carrot Funded Trader Accounts

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is a Carrot Funded Trader Account?">
    Your monetized account after successfully completing both evaluation phases. The profit target is removed. The only rules that remain are the Maximum Daily Loss and Maximum Loss limits. You earn 80% of profits, and your trading may be mirrored into on-chain positions backed by vault capital.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How does the profit split work?">
    As a funded trader, you earn up to 80% of net trading profits. The remaining 20% goes to the OIV (on-chain investment vehicle). All payouts are initiated on-chain and fully verifiable.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are there any ongoing fees for funded traders?">
    No. Once you are funded, there are no recurring platform or subscription fees.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="When and how do I get paid?">
    You can request a payout directly from your Trader Dashboard once you are eligible. Follow these steps:

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Close all trades">
        Ensure all trades — including any pending orders — are fully closed before requesting a payout.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Wait 15 minutes">
        Wait at least 15 minutes after closing your last trade before submitting a payout request.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Request payout">
        Navigate to your Trader Dashboard and submit your payout request. The minimum payout amount is 100 USDC.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Wait for processing">
        Payouts are processed within 24 hours via Arbitrum. Trading is disabled while your payout is being processed. Once the payout is complete, trading is restored and your risk objectives reset.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Warning>
      Double-check your wallet address before submitting a payout request. Payouts sent to incorrect addresses cannot be recovered.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I lose my funded account?">
    Yes. If you breach the Maximum Daily Loss or Maximum Loss thresholds, your funded account will be terminated.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Forbidden trading practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What trading practices are forbidden?">
    The following behaviors are not permitted on carrotfunding.io:

    - **Exploiting technical issues** — taking advantage of bugs, system delays, price feed errors, or execution glitches
    - **Manipulative or coordinated trading** — collusion, using multiple or related accounts, or sharing strategies to game the rules
    - **Unfair automation** — using automated software, HFT systems, or AI-driven tools that provide an unfair advantage
    - **Abusive or unrealistic trading behavior** — extreme overleveraging, one-sided exposure without risk controls, or account flipping
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are the risk management rules?">
    Your trading behavior must remain reasonable and consistent throughout both the evaluation and funded stages. Specifically:

    - You must not suddenly increase position sizes far beyond your historical behavior
    - You must not change your trading patterns in ways that suggest abuse, circumvention, or gambling
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are the account use rules?">
    Accounts are for your personal use only. You may not trade on behalf of others or allow any third party to access your account. Any violation of these rules results in immediate account termination and forfeiture of all payouts.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
