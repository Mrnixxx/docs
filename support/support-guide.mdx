<Note>

</Note>

## Before opening a ticket

Most questions are already answered in the docs. Before reaching out to support, try the following steps:

1. **Search the FAQ** — common questions around evaluations, objectives, payouts, and accounts are covered there.
2. **Read the documentation** — it explains the concepts and rules in detail, and often resolves confusion faster than waiting for a reply.
3. **Still stuck?** Open a ticket via Discord if you cannot find an answer after checking both resources.

## Security reminders

<Warning>
  **Protect your wallet at all times.**

  - Never share your wallet's private key, seed phrase, passwords, or recovery codes with anyone — under any circumstances.
  - carrotfunding support will **never** ask you for sensitive wallet information.
  - Always double-check URLs before connecting your wallet. Scammers frequently use similar-looking domains to impersonate official sites.
  - Only interact with official carrotfunding links shared in the documentation or official Discord channels.
</Warning>
