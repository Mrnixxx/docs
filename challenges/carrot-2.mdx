## Overview

The Carrot 2 challenge is our classic 2-step evaluation. It's designed for traders who want a structured, methodical path to a funded account with realistic, achievable targets.

## Phase parameters

### Phase 1 — Evaluation

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Profit Target | 8% |
| Max Daily Drawdown | 5% |
| Max Total Drawdown | 10% |
| Minimum Trading Days | 3 |
| Time Limit | None |

### Phase 2 — Verification

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Profit Target | 5% |
| Max Daily Drawdown | 5% |
| Max Total Drawdown | 10% |
| Minimum Trading Days | 3 |
| Time Limit | None |

## Funded account conditions

Once you pass both phases, your funded account operates under the following conditions:

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Max Daily Drawdown | 5% |
| Max Total Drawdown | 10% |
| Profit Split | Up to 85% |
| Payout Frequency | Bi-weekly |
| Scaling Available | Yes |

## Example — $25,000 account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Phase 1">
    You need to reach **$27,000** (8% profit = $2,000 gain) without your balance dropping more than **$1,250 in a single day** or **$2,500 total**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Phase 2">
    You need to reach **$26,250** (5% profit = $1,250 gain) under the same drawdown rules.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Funded">
    You receive a $25,000 funded account. Every profit you generate, you keep up to 85%.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  No time limit means you can take your time and trade your strategy without rushing. Quality over quantity.
</Tip>
