## Core rules

These rules apply to all accounts — challenge and funded.

### Drawdown rules

<Warning>
  Violating any drawdown rule results in immediate account termination. There are no warnings.
</Warning>

**Max Daily Drawdown** is calculated from your highest balance of the day (or starting balance if no trades were closed). If your equity drops by the daily drawdown limit at any point during the day, your account is closed.

**Max Total Drawdown** is calculated from your initial account balance (not peak balance). If your equity falls below this threshold at any point, your account is closed.

| | Carrot 2 | Carrot 1 |
|---|---|---|
| Max Daily Drawdown | 5% | 4% |
| Max Total Drawdown | 10% | 8% |

### Minimum trading days

You must trade on a minimum of **3 separate calendar days** before a challenge phase can be completed. Trading on a day means opening and closing at least one position.

### Prohibited behaviour

The following will result in immediate account termination without appeal:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Copy trading / signal services">
    Using third-party signals or copying trades from another account (yours or others) is not permitted during the challenge phase. Funded accounts may use copy trading with prior approval.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Tick scalping / latency arbitrage">
    Strategies that exploit data feed latency, price feed delays, or platform errors are prohibited.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Overnight holding on news events">
    Holding positions open into major scheduled news events (FOMC, NFP, CPI) that could cause extreme slippage is at the trader's risk and may be reviewed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Account sharing">
    Only the registered account owner may trade. Sharing login credentials or having a third party trade on your account is prohibited.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Reverse engineering / exploitation">
    Any attempt to exploit the platform, smart contracts, or pricing mechanisms is prohibited and may result in legal action.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Position sizing

There are no restrictions on position size as long as you stay within the drawdown rules. However, we recommend responsible risk management:

<Tip>
  Most successful funded traders risk between 0.5% and 2% of account balance per trade. Consistency beats big swings.
</Tip>

## Leverage

Maximum leverage varies by asset class. See [Leverage & Fees](/trading/leverage-fees) for a full breakdown.

## Holding over weekends

Positions may be held over weekends. Be aware that crypto markets trade 24/7, while forex and commodities may have gaps at open.

## Consistency rule

There is no formal consistency rule at Carrot Funding. You are free to have large winning days without penalty. We reward performance, not conformity.
