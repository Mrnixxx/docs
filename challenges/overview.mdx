## Pick your path

Carrot Funding offers two challenge types designed for different trader profiles.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Carrot 2" icon="2" href="/challenges/carrot-2">
    **2-Step Evaluation**  
    Lower profit targets across two phases. Ideal for traders who prefer a gradual, lower-pressure path to funding.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Carrot 1" icon="1" href="/challenges/carrot-1">
    **1-Step Evaluation**  
    Single phase with a higher profit target. Best for experienced traders who want to get funded fast.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Account sizes

Both challenge types are available across the following account sizes:

| Account Size | Challenge Fee (Carrot 2) | Challenge Fee (Carrot 1) |
|---|---|---|
| $5,000 | $49 | $59 |
| $10,000 | $89 | $109 |
| $25,000 | $199 | $249 |
| $50,000 | $299 | $379 |
| $100,000 | $499 | $649 |
| $200,000 | $899 | $1,149 |

<Note>
  Challenge fees are refunded on your first funded account payout. You effectively trade for free if you pass.
</Note>

## Which challenge is right for me?

| | Carrot 2 (2-Step) | Carrot 1 (1-Step) |
|---|---|---|
| **Phases** | 2 | 1 |
| **Phase 1 profit target** | 8% | 10% |
| **Phase 2 profit target** | 5% | — |
| **Max daily drawdown** | 5% | 4% |
| **Max total drawdown** | 10% | 8% |
| **Time limit** | None | None |
| **Profit split** | Up to 85% | Up to 90% |
| **Best for** | Consistent traders | Aggressive traders |
