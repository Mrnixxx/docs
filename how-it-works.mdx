## The path to a funded account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your challenge">
    Pick an account size and challenge type that fits your trading style. We offer 1-step (Carrot 1) and 2-step (Carrot 2) evaluation paths starting from $5,000 up to $200,000.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pass the evaluation">
    Trade on gTrade with simulated capital. Hit the profit target while staying within the drawdown rules. No time pressure — trade at your own pace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get funded & earn">
    Once you pass, you receive a funded account with real capital. Trade, earn profits, and receive up to 90% directly to your wallet — on-chain.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens after you pass?

When you successfully complete your challenge phase(s):

1. **Verification** — our system automatically verifies your results on-chain
2. **Funded account issued** — you receive access to your funded trading account
3. **Trade & earn** — profits accumulate and are paid out according to your plan's schedule
4. **Scale up** — consistent performance unlocks higher account sizes

## Key principles

<Info>
  Carrot Funding is non-custodial. We never hold your funds — trading capital is managed through Gains Network's smart contracts on Arbitrum.
</Info>

<Warning>
  Challenge fees are non-refundable unless you pass the challenge, in which case the fee is credited back to you on your first payout.
</Warning>
