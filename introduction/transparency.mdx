The goal is to make every part of the trading and funding process transparent, observable, auditable, and verifiable. You should never have to trust a claim when you can verify it directly on-chain.

<Info>
  All contracts operate on Arbitrum. You can inspect transactions, balances, and execution history at any time using the links below.
</Info>

## What you can verify

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Challenge payments" icon="credit-card">
    Every evaluation fee you pay flows directly into the on-chain system. You can trace the transaction from your wallet to the contract.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Trader payouts" icon="circle-dollar-sign">
    Payouts are executed on-chain. You can verify that funds moved from the vault to your wallet without any intermediary.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Risk management activity" icon="shield-check">
    Both automated and manual hedging activity is executed by known EOAs with constrained permissions. Every action is recorded on-chain.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Your NFT credential" icon="id-card">
    Your challenge NFT is a public, immutable record. Anyone can verify your evaluation parameters and trading history by looking up your token on-chain.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  Bookmark the Arbiscan links above to monitor activity on any contract at any time. You do not need to ask carrotfunding.io for confirmation of any transaction.
</Tip>
