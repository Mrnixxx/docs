## **Carrotfunding.io** vault

The vault is a core component of carrotfunding.io’s decentralized infrastructure, designed to provide capital backing for funded trader accounts with full transparency and risk-managed oversight.

Built on rethink.finance, the vault transparently supports trade execution, risk mitigation, and on-chain payout operations. The NAV (Net Asset Value) is calculated and updated on-chain, offering full verifiability of the protocol's solvency at any given time.
