## The problem with traditional prop firms

Prop trading firms have grown rapidly in popularity, but the industry's growth has outpaced its accountability. Core questions remain unanswered for most traders:

- Are you actually trading the firm's capital, or is everything simulated?
- Are the challenge conditions designed for you to fail?
- Will the firm actually pay out when you hit your targets?

These aren't edge-case concerns — they're structural. Traditional prop firms operate on centralized infrastructure, closed execution environments, and incentive structures that don't always align with your success.

<Warning>
  Several major prop firms have faced insolvency, sudden rule changes, or withheld payouts with little recourse for affected traders. Centralized trust creates centralized risk.
</Warning>

## What carrotfunding.io does differently

### Transparent by design: on-chain business model

Every financial interaction — from your evaluation fee payment to your trader payout — happens on-chain. You don't have to take anyone's word for it. The risk management logic, including how positions are A-booked and B-booked, is part of the on-chain infrastructure and is fully observable. There is no black box.

<Info>
  A-booking routes your positions to live markets for hedging. B-booking retains tail risk internally. Both are visible and auditable on-chain at carrotfunding.io.
</Info>

### DeFi-powered simulated trading environment

Pricing and execution logic come directly from decentralized trading protocols. You trade against the same liquidity and spreads available on those protocols — no artificial markups, no hidden fees. The conditions you evaluate under reflect live decentralized market conditions.

### On-chain track record

Every evaluation you complete is minted as an NFT. This creates a permanent, verifiable record of your trading journey that no one can alter or delete.

Your NFT stores:

- The parameters of your evaluation (rules, targets, drawdown limits)
- Proof of your participation
- Your trading history from the challenge

<Tip>
  You own your NFT. You can transfer it, list it on secondary markets, or use it as proof of trading performance when applying for additional capital or opportunities in the DeFi ecosystem.
</Tip>
