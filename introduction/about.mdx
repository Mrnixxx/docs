carrotfunding.io is the first DeFi-native trader funding firm. It eliminates the trust, transparency, and counterparty risks that have long plagued traditional proprietary trading firms by combining the familiar structure of a performance-based evaluation process with the full transparency of on-chain infrastructure.

<Info>
  Every fund movement — from challenge payments to trader payouts — happens on-chain and in real time. Anyone can verify it.
</Info>

## How it works

You go through a challenge process to prove your trading skills. Each challenge is minted as an NFT that stores your performance data directly on-chain. This NFT serves two purposes: it acts as a collectible and as a verifiable reputation credential. You can trade it on secondary markets, and it becomes a permanent part of your on-chain identity.

Execution is powered by decentralized perpetual futures protocols, so the pricing and liquidity you trade against reflect real market conditions — not a simulated environment controlled by the firm.
