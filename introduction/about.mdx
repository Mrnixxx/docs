## What is carrotfunding.io?

[**carrotfunding.io**](http://carrotfunding.io)\*\* is the first DeFi-native trader funding firm\*\*, built to eliminate the trust, transparency, and counterparty risks that plague traditional proprietary trading firms. We combine the familiar structure of a performance-based evaluation process with the transparency of on-chain infrastructure, perpetual futures liquidity from decentralized protocols, and an NFT-based reputation layer.

Our model serves both new and experienced traders through a\*\* challenge process\*\*. Each challenge is minted as an NFT, storing its performance directly on-chain. This NFT acts as a collectible and reputation credential, tradable on secondary markets and forming part of a trader’s on-chain identity.

Every fund movement, from **challenge payments to trader payouts** happens **on-chain and in real-time**, verifiable by anyone.

> Empowering the most promising traders, fostering innovation with proprietary trading solutions while adhering to exceptional security and transparency standards in the DeFi space.

We believe that the future of trader funding firms is open, transparent, and blockchain-based. Whether you're a DeFi-native, a former CEX, Futures, or CFD trader, or someone just starting their trading journey, [carrotfunding.io](http://carrotfunding.io) offers unmatched transparency in the world of trader funding firms.
