## Leverage limits

Leverage maximums are determined by Gains Network's risk parameters and vary by asset class.

| Asset Class | Min Leverage | Max Leverage |
|---|---|---|
| Crypto (major) | 2x | 150x |
| Crypto (altcoins) | 2x | 50x |
| Forex (major) | 2x | 1000x |
| Forex (minor/exotic) | 2x | 500x |
| Gold / Silver | 2x | 250x |
| Commodities | 2x | 100x |

<Warning>
  High leverage significantly increases the risk of hitting your drawdown limits. Most successful prop traders use 10–50x on crypto and 100–500x on forex.
</Warning>

## Trading fees

All fees are charged by the Gains Network protocol. Carrot Funding does not add additional fees on top.

### Opening & closing fees

| Asset Class | Open Fee | Close Fee |
|---|---|---|
| Crypto | 0.08% | 0.08% |
| Forex | 0.04% | 0.04% |
| Commodities | 0.06% | 0.06% |

Fees are calculated on **position size (notional value)**, not on margin.

**Example:** Opening a $10,000 BTC position (e.g. $100 margin at 100x) → fee = $10,000 × 0.08% = **$8**

### Borrowing fee (overnight)

Positions held open accrue a borrowing fee charged per block on Arbitrum. The rate varies by asset and current open interest utilization.

You can view the live borrowing rate for any asset in the trading terminal before placing a trade.

### Liquidation

A position is liquidated when unrealized losses reach **90% of the margin**. With mandatory Stop Losses, liquidation should never be reached in normal trading.

## Fee impact on drawdown

<Tip>
  Fees count toward your drawdown. On a $25,000 account with 5% daily drawdown ($1,250), high-frequency trading with large positions can eat into your daily limit quickly. Factor fees into your daily risk budget.
</Tip>
