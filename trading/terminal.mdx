The carrotfunding.io trading terminal is developed in-house to give you a seamless, secure interface for executing trades. Whether you're refining your strategy or operating within defined risk constraints, the terminal puts full control and real-time visibility in your hands.

The interface is clean and responsive, optimized for trading across both desktop and mobile.

## Key features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Decentralized execution" icon="network-wired">
    Trades reflect live conditions from integrated decentralized trading protocols — including real-time pricing, slippage, spreads, and execution logic. There are no artificial markups on fees or spreads.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Professional charting" icon="chart-line">
    Intuitive design powered by TradingView charting and advanced order management tools give you everything you need to analyze markets and manage positions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Real-time risk monitoring" icon="shield-halved">
    Your equity limits and consistency score are always visible while you trade, so you can stay within challenge rules without losing focus on the market.
  </Card>
  <Card title="In-house terminal" icon="display">
    Built and maintained by carrotfunding.io — not a third-party embed. The terminal is designed around the evaluation experience, not retrofitted for it.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Accessing the terminal

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your dashboard">
    Log in to carrotfunding.io and navigate to your Trader Dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your challenge">
    Locate your active challenge and click **Trade** next to it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start trading">
    The terminal launches directly in your browser — no downloads or plugins required.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  The terminal is available on mobile. If you trade on the go, the interface adapts to your screen without losing any core functionality.
</Tip>
