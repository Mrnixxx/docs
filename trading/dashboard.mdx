The Trader Dashboard is your command center for monitoring performance, tracking challenge progress, and managing your evaluation journey. All the information you need is surfaced in one place so you can focus on execution.

## What you can do from the dashboard

- Manage your active challenges and associated NFTs
- View your account status and current stage (Evaluation, Verification, or Funded)
- Track key trading objectives and your progress toward completing them
- Review performance metrics and daily performance breakdowns
- Manage your profile and submit payout requests
- Access evaluation-level analytics and drawdown thresholds

<Info>
  Dashboard data refreshes automatically every 30 seconds, so your metrics are always up to date.
</Info>

## Equity limits

Your equity limits are always visible on the dashboard:

- **Maximum Daily Loss Limit** — the maximum amount you can lose in a single trading day
- **Maximum Loss Limit** — the overall drawdown threshold for your account

A reset timer is displayed alongside both limits so you always know when calculations reset.

## Requesting a payout

Before submitting a payout request, make sure your account meets the eligibility requirements. Trading is disabled while your payout is being processed and will be restored once it completes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Close all trades">
    Ensure every position and pending order is fully closed. Open or pending trades will prevent the payout request from being submitted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait 15 minutes">
    After closing your last trade, wait at least 15 minutes before proceeding. This gives the system time to finalize your account state.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the request">
    Navigate to your dashboard and submit the payout request. The minimum payout amount is **100 USDC**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for processing">
    Trading is disabled while your payout is being processed. Once the payout completes, trading access is automatically restored.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Double-check your wallet address before submitting. Payouts sent to an incorrect wallet address cannot be recovered. carrotfunding.io cannot reverse or redirect transactions once submitted.
</Warning>
