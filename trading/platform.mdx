## Powered by Gains Network

Carrot Funding runs on **[gTrade](https://gains.trade)** — the leading decentralized perpetuals exchange built on Arbitrum. All trading happens on-chain, giving you full transparency over your positions, fees, and P&L.

<Info>
  gTrade has processed billions in trading volume and is one of the most battle-tested DeFi perpetuals protocols in existence.
</Info>

## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your wallet">
    Connect a Web3 wallet (MetaMask, Rabby, or any WalletConnect-compatible wallet) to the Carrot Funding platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Switch to Arbitrum">
    Ensure your wallet is connected to the **Arbitrum One** network. If not, the platform will prompt you to switch.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access your account">
    After purchasing your challenge, your funded trading account will be linked to your wallet address. Navigate to the Terminal to start trading.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Place a trade">
    Select an asset, set your position size, choose leverage, set your Stop Loss and Take Profit levels, and confirm the transaction.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Stop Loss requirement

<Warning>
  A Stop Loss is **mandatory** on all positions. Orders submitted without a Stop Loss will be rejected by the platform.
</Warning>

Your Stop Loss must be set at or before your Max Daily Drawdown threshold to protect your account. The platform will warn you if your SL would breach your drawdown limit.
