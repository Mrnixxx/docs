## Trading hours

- **Crypto:** 24/7, 365 days a year
- **Forex:** Sunday 22:00 UTC to Friday 22:00 UTC
- **Commodities:** Varies by asset — check the terminal for session times

## Liquidity

All trades are executed against the **GNS liquidity vault** on Arbitrum — no order book, no counterparty risk, and no slippage on entries (prices are oracle-based).

<Info>
  gTrade uses Chainlink price oracles for settlement, ensuring fair and manipulation-resistant pricing.
</Info>
