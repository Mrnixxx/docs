At carrotfunding.io, every trader starts with a structured, rule-based challenge designed to assess real trading skill under fully transparent conditions — no hidden metrics, no guesswork.

The objective: demonstrate consistency, disciplined risk management, and profitability across two evaluation phases before receiving a funded account.

---

## The Carrot 2-phase challenge

<Steps>
  <Step title="Phase 1: Evaluation account">
    Trade in a simulated environment and hit your profit target while staying within all drawdown limits and risk rules.

    To complete this phase, you must:

    - Reach the profit target using closed positions only
    - Stay within the maximum daily loss limit
    - Stay within the maximum loss limit
    - Achieve a consistency score of ≥70
    - Close all open positions before the target is considered met

    Once you complete all trading objectives, your **Verification Account is instantly created** and available in your dashboard — no delays.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Phase 2: Verification account">
    The final step before becoming a funded trader. Trading objectives in this phase are intentionally more relaxed — the profit target is reduced and there is still no time limit.

    <Note>
      There is no time limit on the Verification phase. Focus on discipline and execution while staying within the risk rules.
    </Note>

    After you complete all objectives, the carrotfunding.io team reviews your performance. This review typically takes **1–2 business days**. Your **Carrot Funded Trader Account** is then created and available in your Trader Dashboard.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Carrot Funded Trader Account">
    Once funded, the profit target is removed. You only need to stay within the remaining risk rules.

    **What changes:**
    - Profit target removed
    - Maximum daily loss and maximum loss limits remain in effect
    - **80% profit split** — 80% of profits go to you

    **Payouts:**
    - All trading activity is backed by capital from the on-chain vault
    - All payouts are initiated on-chain and are fully verifiable
    - Minimum payout: 100 USDC
    - All trades, including pending orders, must be fully closed before requesting a payout
    - Wait at least **15 minutes** after closing your last trade before submitting a payout request
    - Payouts are processed within **24 hours** via Arbitrum

    <Info>
      While a payout is being processed, trading is automatically disabled. Once your payout is completed, trading access is restored and all risk objectives reset to their original limits.
    </Info>

    <Warning>
      Verify your wallet address carefully before submitting a payout request. Payouts sent to an incorrect wallet address cannot be recovered.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Business model

carrotfunding.io operates with full transparency into how revenue flows and how funded traders are paid.

### Revenue allocation

When you purchase an evaluation plan, revenue is split as follows:

| Allocation | Recipient | Purpose |
|------------|-----------|---------|
| 50% | carrotfunding.io | Technology infrastructure, operational overhead, and platform development |
| 50% | On-chain vault (rethink.finance) | Backs funded traders' accounts and supports trader payouts |

### Profit distribution

Once you become a funded trader, and if internal performance criteria indicate a strong probability of continued profitability, profits are distributed as follows:

| Share | Recipient |
|-------|-----------|
| 80% | You (the trader) |
| 20% | The on-chain vault |

### Vault management

carrotfunding.io acts as fund manager of the on-chain vault with the following fee structure, both subject to a high-water mark:

- **1%** management fee
- **20%** performance fee
