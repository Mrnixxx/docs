## Set up your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit carrotfunding.io">
    Go to [carrotfunding.io](https://www.carrotfunding.io/) and click **Get Funded** or **Enter App**.

    <Frame>
      <img
        src="/images/docs-homepage.png"
        alt="Docs Homepage"
        title="Docs Homepage"
        className="mr-auto"
        style={{ width:"65%" }}
      />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Choose the sign-up method that works best for you.

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Wallet">
        Click **Sign in with Wallet**, then approve the signature request in your wallet extension (MetaMask, Rabby, or any other supported wallet).

        <Info>
          This signature only verifies wallet ownership. No gas fees or on-chain transactions are involved.
        </Info>
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Google">
        Click **Sign in with Google**, select your preferred Google account, and approve the requested permissions. You'll be redirected to your dashboard automatically.
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Email">
        Click **Sign up** at the bottom of the sign-in page, fill in your details, click **Continue**, then confirm your email address.
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in">
    Sign in using the same method you chose during sign-up.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Start a new challenge

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to New Challenge">
    After logging in, you'll be taken to the **New Challenge** page automatically. If you already have an active challenge, click **New Challenge** in the navigation bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your challenge plan">
    Select your preferred **Leverage Mode** (Normal or Aggressive) and your **Account Size**. The summary panel updates in real time to show:

    - Maximum position size
    - Profit target
    - Maximum daily loss
    - Maximum loss
    - Profit split

    Challenge parameters adjust automatically based on the leverage mode you select.

    Once you're satisfied with your configuration, choose your preferred payment method (crypto or fiat), accept the Terms & Conditions, and click **Confirm & Proceed to Payment**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete payment">
    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Card payment">
        You'll be redirected to **Wert**, a Web2 payment provider. Pay with Visa, Mastercard, Apple Pay, or Google Pay.
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Crypto payment">
        You'll be redirected to **DePay**, a Web3 payment provider. Select your preferred crypto wallet and confirm the transaction.
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start trading">
    After payment is confirmed, you'll be redirected to your **Trader Dashboard**. Click **Trade** to launch the trading terminal and begin your challenge.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Trading objectives

Every challenge is guided by clear, transparent trading objectives designed to assess consistency, discipline, and risk management.

### Key definitions

- **Balance**: Total account value based on closed trades (realized PnL), excluding open positions.
- **Equity**: Total account value including both closed trades (realized PnL) and open trades (unrealized PnL).
- **Equity limit**: The level of equity at which a breach is automatically triggered.
- **Breach**: Automatic closure and cancellation of all positions and active orders. The challenge account is terminated.
- **Maximum daily loss limit**: Daily equity limit, calculated at 00:00:00 UTC each day based on your account balance at that time.
- **Maximum loss limit**: Overall equity limit for the challenge account.
- **High-water mark**: The highest account balance recorded during the challenge.

### Trading period

<Note>
  There is no time limit on your challenge. Trade at your own pace — your trading period is unlimited.
</Note>

### Consistency score

Your consistency score must be **≥70** to progress to the next phase or request a payout. This ensures no single trading day represents more than 30% of your total profit.

**Formula:**

```text
Consistency Score = (1 - |most profitable or losing day| / |result of all trading days|) × 100
```

**Example:**

- Total profit: 10,000 USDC. Most profitable day: 3,500 USDC.
- Score = (1 - 3,500 / 10,000) × 100 = **65** — not yet passing.
- You add 2,000 more profit. Total: 12,000 USDC.
- Score = (1 - 3,500 / 12,000) × 100 = **71** — passing.

<Tip>
  A score below 70 does not fail your challenge. Continue trading until your best single day accounts for less than 30% of your total profit.
</Tip>

### Maximum daily loss

Your maximum daily loss is **5% of your account balance** measured at 00:00:00 UTC. This limit applies to both realized and unrealized losses and resets at the start of each new trading day.

### Maximum loss

Your maximum loss is **10% from your high-water mark balance**. This limit is recalculated — not reset — each time you record a new highest balance. It stops trailing once the maximum loss limit would exceed your initial starting balance.

### Profit target

- **Evaluation phase**: 8% of initial account balance (Normal) or 10% (Aggressive).
- The target is measured using closed positions only — unrealized profits do not count.
- All open positions must be closed before the target is considered met.
