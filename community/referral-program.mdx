The referral program rewards you for inviting traders with lifetime revenue share across multiple streams — from challenges sold and from profit splits on funded trader payouts. Referrals also qualify you for [Carrot Points](/community/points-program).

## How referrals work

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in or sign up">
    Log in to your account at [app.carrotfunding.io](https://app.carrotfunding.io/). If you don't have an account yet, create one to get started.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your unique referral code">
    Go to the **Referrals** page in your dashboard and generate your unique referral code. Share it anywhere — socials, Discord, direct message.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start earning">
    Every time a trader signs up using your code and purchases a challenge, you both benefit:

    - **You earn:** 10% lifetime revenue share from their challenge purchases and funded trader profit splits
    - **They receive:** 10% off their first challenge
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get paid on-chain">
    All referral rewards are settled transparently on-chain via your Arbitrum wallet.

    - Payouts processed within **24 hours**
    - Minimum payout threshold: **$100 USDC**
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Revenue share applies to both challenge purchases **and** funded trader payouts — so your earnings grow as your referred traders advance and receive profit splits.
</Note>
