Carrot Points measure your contribution, performance, and activity in the carrotfunding.io ecosystem. You can track your standing on the leaderboard and compete for exclusive rewards.

<Note>
  Carrot Points may be used in the future for an airdrop allocation. Accumulating points now means you are building a verifiable record of participation — making them a key part of long-term engagement with the platform.
</Note>

## Ways to earn points

| Action | Points earned |
|--------|--------------|
| Mint an Access NFT* | 2 🥕 per USDC spent |
| Pass Challenge* | 3 🥕 per USDC spent |
| Pass Verification* | 4 🥕 per USDC spent |
| Receive a payout | 5 🥕 per USDC earned |
| Referral revenue** | 0.5 🥕 per USDC earned |
| Growth Partner revenue*** | 1 🥕 per USDC earned |

\* Based on evaluation purchase price  
\*\* Recurring from referred traders  
\*\*\* Recurring for Growth Partners

<Tip>
  The highest point multipliers are tied to advancing through evaluations and receiving funded payouts — so the most effective way to accumulate points is to trade well and progress through the platform.
</Tip>

## Leaderboard

Your Carrot Points total is publicly tracked on the leaderboard. Top performers qualify for exclusive rewards announced by the carrotfunding.io team. Check the leaderboard regularly in your dashboard to see where you rank.
