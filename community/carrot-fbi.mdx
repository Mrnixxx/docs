The Carrot Funded Bunny Initiative (FBI) is a dynamic NFT collection that merges visual collectibles with real trading evaluation data. Each NFT acts as both a piece of digital art and a progress badge, reflecting your performance and reputation within the carrotfunding.io ecosystem.

**Objectives:**
- Build an on-chain identity layer for traders
- Combine rarity-based visual traits with dynamic trading metadata
- Turn NFTs into living records of your evaluation progress, profit milestones, and platform status

## NFT structure

Carrot Funded Bunny NFTs are composed of two core property types: evaluation properties that update dynamically alongside your trading data, and visual rarity properties that are assigned at mint.

<Info>
  **Dynamic metadata** means the on-chain properties of your NFT update automatically as your trading journey progresses. When you advance from Challenge to Verification, reach a new profit milestone, or scale your account size, your NFT reflects those changes in real time — no manual updates required.
</Info>

### Evaluation properties (dynamic)

These traits are linked to your live trader data and updated on-chain as your account progresses.

| Property | Details |
|----------|---------|
| Leverage mode | Normal / Aggressive |
| Account size | $5k / $10k / $25k / $50k / $100k / $200k |
| Stage | Challenge / Verification / Funded / Inactive |
