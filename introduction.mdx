## What is Carrot Funding?

Carrot Funding is a **DeFi-native prop firm** built on [gTrade (Gains Network)](https://gains.trade) perpetuals on Arbitrum. We provide traders with funded accounts to trade crypto, forex, and commodities — fully on-chain, with transparent rules and instant payouts.

Unlike traditional prop firms, everything at Carrot Funding runs on-chain. No hidden rules, no opaque risk desks, no delayed withdrawals.
