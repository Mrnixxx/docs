## Get started in 4 steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [app.carrotfunding.io](https://app.carrotfunding.io/) using your wallet, Google, or email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Purchase a challenge">
    Choose your account size ($5k–$50k) and leverage mode (Normal or Aggressive). Pay with crypto or card.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Hit your trading objectives">
    Complete the Evaluation and Verification phases by meeting profit targets while respecting risk limits.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Trade funded and get paid">
    Receive your Carrot Funded Trader Account, trade without a profit target, and request on-chain payouts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Every challenge is minted as an NFT, creating a permanent, verifiable record of your trading journey on-chain.
</Note>
