## General

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Carrot Funding?">
    Carrot Funding is a DeFi-native proprietary trading firm. We provide traders with funded accounts to trade perpetuals on Gains Network (gTrade) on Arbitrum. You pass a challenge, we provide capital, you keep up to 90% of the profits.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is Carrot Funding a real prop firm?">
    Yes. Unlike some platforms that simulate trading, Carrot Funding operates with real on-chain capital through Gains Network's smart contracts on Arbitrum. All positions, fees, and payouts are verifiable on-chain.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need crypto experience to use Carrot Funding?">
    You need a basic understanding of Web3 wallets (MetaMask or similar) and Arbitrum. Trading experience with perpetuals is strongly recommended before attempting a challenge.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Which countries are supported?">
    Carrot Funding is accessible globally. However, it is your responsibility to ensure that participating in a prop firm challenge and trading perpetuals is legal in your jurisdiction.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Challenges

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is there a time limit on challenges?">
    No. There is no time limit on any challenge phase. You can take as long as you need to hit your profit target, as long as you do not violate the drawdown rules.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I retry a failed challenge?">
    Yes. You can purchase a new challenge at any time. If you fail due to a drawdown violation, the challenge fee is not refunded — but you are free to start again.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I trade on weekends?">
    Yes. Crypto markets trade 24/7, so crypto positions can be opened and closed at any time. Forex and commodity markets have session hours — check the terminal for live session status.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I hold positions overnight or over the weekend?">
    Yes. There are no restrictions on holding positions overnight or over weekends. Be aware that borrowing fees accrue on open positions, and weekend gaps can occur on forex pairs.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are EAs (Expert Advisors) / bots allowed?">
    Automated trading is allowed provided it does not exploit platform vulnerabilities, use latency arbitrage, or violate any other trading rules. You are responsible for the trading activity on your account regardless of whether it was executed manually or by a bot.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Payouts

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I receive my payout?">
    Payouts are sent automatically to your connected wallet address on Arbitrum in USDC or DAI. There is no withdrawal request — it happens automatically at each bi-weekly payout cycle.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if I have a losing payout period?">
    If you have a net loss in a payout cycle, no payout is issued. The loss is carried forward and must be recovered before future profits are paid out.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my challenge fee refunded?">
    Yes — your challenge fee is refunded on your first funded account payout, provided you pass the challenge.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Technical

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What network do I need?">
    Arbitrum One (Chain ID: 42161). The platform will prompt you to switch if you're on the wrong network.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What wallets are supported?">
    Any EVM-compatible wallet works — MetaMask, Rabby, Coinbase Wallet, or any WalletConnect wallet.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are my funds safe?">
    Trading capital is managed through Gains Network's audited smart contracts. Carrot Funding does not hold your funds directly. However, DeFi protocols carry inherent smart contract risk — trade responsibly.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
