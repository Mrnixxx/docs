## What we collect

- **Wallet address** — required to link your trading account and process payouts
- **Email address** — for account notifications and support
- **Trading activity** — all on-chain activity is publicly visible on Arbitrum by nature of blockchain technology

## What we don't collect

- We do not collect payment card details (all transactions are on-chain)
- We do not sell your data to third parties
- We do not use your data for advertising

## On-chain data

By the nature of blockchain, all trading activity associated with your wallet is permanently and publicly recorded on Arbitrum. This is not within Carrot Funding's control.
