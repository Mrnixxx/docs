## Base profit splits

| Challenge | Starting Split |
|---|---|
| Carrot 2 Funded | 80% |
| Carrot 1 Funded | 85% |

Your split increases automatically as you hit performance milestones. See the [Scaling Plan](/payouts/scaling) for details.

## Maximum profit splits

| Challenge | Maximum Split |
|---|---|
| Carrot 2 Funded | 85% |
| Carrot 1 Funded | 90% |

## What counts as "profit"?

Net profit is calculated as:

```
Net Profit = Realized Gains − Realized Losses − Trading Fees − Borrowing Fees
```

Only **closed** positions count toward profit. Unrealized gains/losses on open positions are not included in payout calculations.
