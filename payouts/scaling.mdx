## Overview

Carrot Funding's scaling plan rewards consistent, disciplined traders with larger account sizes and higher profit splits. As you demonstrate profitability over time, we increase your capital allocation automatically.

## How scaling works

After each payout cycle, your performance is reviewed. If you meet the scaling criteria, your account balance and/or profit split increases for the next cycle.

### Scaling criteria

To qualify for a scale-up at each review:

- Minimum **3 profitable payout cycles** consecutively
- No drawdown rule violations in the review period
- Net profit of at least **10% of account balance** over the review period

### Scale-up milestones

| Milestone | Account Increase | Profit Split Increase |
|---|---|---|
| 1st scale-up | +25% account size | +2.5% |
| 2nd scale-up | +25% account size | +2.5% |
| 3rd scale-up | +25% account size | Max split reached |

**Example — $25,000 Carrot 2 account:**

| Stage | Account Size | Profit Split |
|---|---|---|
| Initial | $25,000 | 80% |
| After 1st scale | $31,250 | 82.5% |
| After 2nd scale | $39,062 | 85% |
| After 3rd scale | $48,828 | 85% (max) |

## Maximum account size

The maximum funded account size through scaling is **$400,000** regardless of starting tier.

<Tip>
  The fastest path to maximum capital is consistent profitability with controlled drawdown — not chasing big wins. Traders who scale up do so by compounding steady returns.
</Tip>

## Scaling resets

A drawdown rule violation during a funded account phase will reset your scaling progress. Your account size returns to the original funded amount and your split returns to the base rate.
