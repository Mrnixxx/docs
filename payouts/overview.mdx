## Profit split

Carrot Funding offers industry-leading profit splits:

| Challenge Type | Profit Split |
|---|---|
| Carrot 2 (2-Step) | Up to 85% |
| Carrot 1 (1-Step) | Up to 90% |

Your profit split starts at the base rate and can increase through our [Scaling Plan](/payouts/scaling).

## Payout schedule

Payouts are processed **bi-weekly** (every 14 days) directly to your connected wallet address on Arbitrum.

<Info>
  Because everything runs on-chain, there is no withdrawal request process. Profits are distributed automatically to your wallet at each payout cycle.
</Info>

## How payouts are calculated

At each payout date:

1. Your **net realized profit** since the last payout is calculated
2. Your profit split percentage is applied
3. The amount is sent directly to your wallet in **USDC or DAI**
4. Your account resets for the next payout cycle

**Example:** You earn $3,000 in net profit on a Carrot 1 funded account with a 90% split → you receive **$2,700 to your wallet**.

## First payout & fee refund

<Note>
  Your challenge fee is refunded on your **first funded account payout**. If you paid $199 for a $25,000 Carrot 2 challenge, your first payout will include the $199 refund on top of your profit share.
</Note>

## Withdrawal minimum

There is no minimum withdrawal amount. Any net profit accumulated during a payout cycle will be distributed.

## Tax responsibility

Carrot Funding does not withhold taxes. You are responsible for reporting and paying any applicable taxes on your earnings in your jurisdiction. All on-chain transactions are publicly verifiable.
